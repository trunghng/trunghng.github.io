## The exponential family
{: #exp-fam}
The **exponential family** of distributions of $x$ given a measure $\eta$, is defined as family of distribution of form
\begin{equation}
p(x\vert\eta)=h(x)\exp\Big[\eta^\\Big]
\end{equation}
